MOVIE GALLERY

The Equalizer

Film by Antoine Fuqua

A man who believes he has put his mysterious past behind him cannot stand idly by when he meets a young girl under the control of ultra-violent Russian gangsters.

Rating 7.2/10

Free Guy

Film by Shawn Levy

When Guy, a bank teller, learns that he is a non-player character in a bloodthirsty, open-world video game, he goes on to become the hero of the story and takes the responsibility of saving the world.

Rating 7.1/10

Léon: The Professional

Film by Luc Besson

Leon (played by Jean Reno) lives in New York and is a professional hit man. One of his neighbors, Mathilda (Natalie Portman), is a 12-year old girl with family problems, one of which is that her father is a petty criminal. One day, a drug kingpin, Stansfield (Gary Oldman), kills her entire family due to something her father did. Mathilda turns to Leon for help...and revenge.

Rating 8.5/10

Man on Fire

Film by Tony Scott

A former assassin swears vengeance on the kidnappers of the girl he is hired to protect.

Rating 7.7/10

Polar

Film by Jonas Åkerlund

A retiring assassin suddenly finds himself on the receiving end of a hit, contracted by none other than his own employer seeking to cash in on the pensions of aging employees.

Rating 6.3/10
